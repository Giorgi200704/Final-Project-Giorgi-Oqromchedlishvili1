Bakuriani ski resort

  • Home / Ski resorts in Georgia / Bakuriani ski resort

History

Bakuriani is the country oldest winter resort, with two centuries of history. Today, it is a world-class mountain & ski center with the perfect environment for Winter Olympic sports and amateur skiers alike in the colder months, and extreme mountain sports or family-friendly vacations in the summer.

The Location of Bakuriani

Bakuriani Ski Resort is located 1,700 meters above sea level, in the Borjomi Municipality of southern Georgia. It is 180 kilometers from Tbilisi, and you can get there by car or by minibus. If you are headed to Bakuriani from Borjomi, there is a small railway where you will find the “kukushka” train, which will take you 37 kilometers through the lovely Borjomistskali River valley. There is also a railway bridge here that was built based on plans drawn up by Gustave Eiffel!
